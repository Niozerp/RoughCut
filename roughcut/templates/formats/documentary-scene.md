# Documentary Scene Format

A narrative-driven format designed for documentary storytelling with emphasis on emotional beats and contextual depth.

## Structure Overview

This format creates compelling documentary scenes that balance information delivery with emotional resonance.

## Timing Specifications

### Segment 1: Establishing Shot (0:00 - 0:10)
- **Duration**: 10 seconds
- **Purpose**: Set the scene and establish location/mood
- **Content**: Wide establishing shot or B-roll context
- **Music**: Ambient bed or location-appropriate texture

### Segment 2: Introduction (0:10 - 0:45)
- **Duration**: 35 seconds
- **Purpose**: Introduce subject and core theme
- **Content**: Opening narration or subject introduction
- **Music**: Subtle underscore building anticipation

### Segment 3: Narrative Development (0:45 - 2:30)
- **Duration**: 1 minute 45 seconds
- **Purpose**: Deep dive into the story
- **Content**: Interview segments, supporting B-roll, contextual footage
- **Music**: Emotional bed that follows story arc

### Segment 4: Climax/Moment (2:30 - 3:00)
- **Duration**: 30 seconds
- **Purpose**: Emotional peak or key revelation
- **Content**: Pivotal moment, key quote, or dramatic reveal
- **Music**: Swell to support emotional weight

### Segment 5: Resolution (3:00 - 3:30)
- **Duration**: 30 seconds
- **Purpose**: Provide closure and context
- **Content**: Reflective moment, summary, or forward-looking statement
- **Music**: Resolve to neutral bed or fade

## Asset Groups

### Music
- **establishing_texture**: Ambient location bed (atmospheric, subtle)
- **intro_underscore**: Subtle anticipation music (minimal, building)
- **emotional_bed**: Story-following emotional music (cinematic, dynamic)
- **climax_swell**: Emotional peak support (dramatic, swelling)
- **resolution_fade**: Closure music (soft, resolving)

### SFX
- **location_tone**: Room tone and environmental ambience (natural, immersive)
- **transition_drift**: Soft scene transitions (subtle, seamless)

### VFX
- **location_title**: Location identifier graphic (subtle, elegant)
- **archival_frame**: Historical photo treatment (aged, contextual)
- **chapter_marker**: Scene transition indicator (minimal, clean)

## Ideal Use Cases

- Character profiles
- Historical segments
- Issue-based documentaries
- Behind-the-scenes features

## Technical Notes

- Total runtime: ~3:30
- Audio: Layered approach with room tone, dialogue, music, and subtle SFX
- VFX: Minimal - focus on clean cuts and natural transitions
- Pacing: Slower than corporate formats, allowing moments to breathe
