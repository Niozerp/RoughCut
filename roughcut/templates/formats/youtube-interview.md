# YouTube Interview — Corporate Format

This format creates engaging interview content optimized for YouTube retention and professional corporate presentation.

## Structure Overview

The format follows a three-act structure designed to hook viewers quickly, deliver value in the narrative, and end with a clear call-to-action.

## Timing Specifications

### Segment 1: Hook (0:00 - 0:15)
- **Duration**: 15 seconds
- **Purpose**: Grab attention immediately with dynamic energy
- **Content**: Strong opening statement or compelling question
- **Music**: Upbeat corporate intro music

### Segment 2: Narrative (0:15 - 3:15)
- **Duration**: 3 minutes
- **Purpose**: Deliver core interview content with supporting context
- **Content**: Main story arc with natural conversation flow
- **Music**: Subtle background bed music

### Segment 3: Outro (3:15 - 3:45)
- **Duration**: 30 seconds
- **Purpose**: Reinforce message with call-to-action
- **Content**: Summary, subscribe prompt, end screen
- **Music**: Branded outro sting with swell

## Asset Groups

### Music
- **intro_music**: High-energy corporate music (upbeat, professional)
- **narrative_bed**: Subtle background music (corporate, neutral)
- **outro_music**: Branded outro sting with swell (triumphant, branded)

### SFX
- **intro_whoosh**: Subtle entrance sound for title card (swish, airy)
- **outro_chime**: Success sound for call-to-action (bright, positive)

### VFX
- **lower_third_intro**: Speaker name and title at 0:15 (clean, corporate)
- **subscribe_animation**: End screen CTA graphics at 3:30 (animated, branded)

## Ideal Use Cases

- Executive interviews
- Client testimonials
- Industry expert discussions
- Company culture showcases

## Technical Notes

- Total runtime: ~3:45
- Audio: Dialogue on primary track, music on secondary, SFX on tertiary
- VFX: Lower thirds for speaker identification at 0:15
