# Social Media Short Format

High-energy, fast-paced format designed for TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and other social platforms.

## Structure Overview

This format prioritizes immediate engagement and maintains attention through quick cuts, dynamic pacing, and punchy content delivery.

## Timing Specifications

### Segment 1: Hook (0:00 - 0:03)
- **Duration**: 3 seconds
- **Purpose**: Stop the scroll with immediate impact
- **Content**: Bold statement, surprising visual, or compelling question
- **Music**: High-energy intro hit or catchy hook

### Segment 2: Quick Setup (0:03 - 0:10)
- **Duration**: 7 seconds
- **Purpose**: Establish context rapidly
- **Content**: On-screen text, quick explanation, or visual demonstration
- **Music**: Driving beat continues

### Segment 3: Main Content (0:10 - 0:45)
- **Duration**: 35 seconds
- **Purpose**: Deliver the core value/message
- **Content**: Tips, story, demonstration, or reaction
- **Music**: Upbeat track with energy maintained
- **Pacing**: Quick cuts every 2-5 seconds

### Segment 4: Payoff/Conclusion (0:45 - 0:55)
- **Duration**: 10 seconds
- **Purpose**: Deliver satisfying conclusion or call-to-action
- **Content**: Result reveal, final tip, or follow request
- **Music**: Punchy outro or resolve

### Segment 5: Outro Card (0:55 - 1:00)
- **Duration**: 5 seconds
- **Purpose**: Brand recognition and follow prompt
- **Content**: Logo, handle display, or subscribe animation
- **Music**: Fade or signature sound

## Asset Groups

### Music
- **hook_hit**: High-energy intro sound (catchy, trending)
- **driving_beat**: Energetic background track (upbeat, modern)
- **outro_punch**: Satisfying conclusion sound (punchy, memorable)
- **signature_sound**: Brand audio identifier (short, recognizable)

### SFX
- **scroll_stop**: Impact sound for hook (boom, whoosh)
- **transition_swipe**: Quick cut transition (swish, zip)
- **pop_reveal**: Element appearance sound (pop, ding)
- **follow_prompt**: Call-to-action sound (chime, bell)

### VFX
- **hook_text**: Bold opening text overlay (large, animated)
- **setup_caption**: Context text elements (clean, readable)
- **cut_transition**: Fast transition effect (dynamic, energetic)
- **outro_card**: Follow/subscribe animation (branded, animated)

## Platform-Specific Notes

### Vertical (9:16)
- Safe zones: Keep text within center 1080x1350
- Captions: Large, high-contrast subtitles recommended
- Hook placement: Center screen for immediate visibility

### Horizontal (16:9 - YouTube Shorts)
- Utilize full frame with dynamic framing
- Center primary subject for mobile viewing
- B-roll can fill frame edges

## Ideal Use Cases

- Quick tips and tutorials
- Day-in-the-life segments
- Reaction content
- Before/after reveals
- Trending sound participation

## Technical Notes

- Total runtime: 15-60 seconds (optimal: 30-45s)
- Audio: Dialogue primary, music secondary, SFX for emphasis
- Captions: Required for social platforms
- Text on screen: Large, readable, 3-second minimum duration
- Pacing: Cut every 2-5 seconds to maintain engagement
