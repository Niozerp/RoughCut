# Structure Overview

A fast-paced social media short format optimized for mobile viewing. Designed for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts with quick cuts and high energy throughout.

## Timing Specifications

### Hook (0:00 - 0:03)
**Duration**: 3 seconds
**Purpose**: Instant attention grab - users scroll fast!

- Start with strongest visual or audio hook
- No intro music fade - hit hard immediately
- Consider starting mid-sentence for intrigue
- Pattern interrupt to stop the scroll

### Content Body (0:03 - 0:25)
**Duration**: 22 seconds
**Purpose**: Deliver core value/message with rapid pacing

- Cut every 2-5 seconds maximum
- Jump cuts acceptable and expected
- Music: high-energy, trending beat
- Text overlays for key points
- Quick B-roll inserts on keywords

### CTA/Outro (0:25 - 0:30)
**Duration**: 5 seconds
**Purpose**: Call-to-action before user scrolls away

- Quick, punchy call-to-action
- Follow/subscribe prompt
- End card or branded outro
- Music drop/hit on final frame

## Asset Groups

### Music
- **hook_hit**: Instant impact sound for opening (impact, punchy, short, trending)
- **content_beat**: High-energy music bed for body (energetic, trending, upbeat, fast)
- **outro_hit**: Final impact sound (impact, short, memorable, branded)

### SFX
- **transition_swipe**: Quick swipe transition (swipe, fast, transition, modern)
- **notification_pop**: Pop sound for text overlays (pop, notification, modern, clean)
- **whoosh_fast**: Fast whoosh for emphasis (whoosh, fast, energetic)

### VFX
- **text_overlay_hook**: Bold text for hook (text_overlay, bold, modern, animated)
- **text_overlay_point**: Point text overlays (text_overlay, clean, readable, punchy)
- **end_card**: Branded end card (end_card, branded, clean)

## Cutting Rules

- Cut every 2-5 seconds maximum - never hold a shot longer
- Use jump cuts freely - they're expected in short-form
- Remove all "ums", "ahs", and dead air ruthlessly
- Start hook mid-sentence if it creates intrigue
- Add text overlay on every key point (audio + visual reinforcement)
- Music must be trending/energetic - no slow tracks
- Total runtime: exactly 30 seconds for Shorts/Reels
- End before user has time to scroll (beat the algorithm)
- Consider vertical 9:16 or square 1:1 framing in template notes
