# Structure Overview

A cinematic documentary scene format that follows classic storytelling structure. Designed for vérité and interview-based documentaries with a clear narrative arc.

## Timing Specifications

### Setup (0:00 - 0:45)
**Duration**: 45 seconds
**Purpose**: Establish context, setting, and characters

- Open with environmental/establishing shots or ambient sound
- Introduce subject or scene context
- Set emotional tone with appropriate music bed
- Build anticipation for what's to come

### Conflict/Tension (0:45 - 2:30)
**Duration**: 1 minute 45 seconds
**Purpose**: Present the core challenge, tension, or turning point

- Peak emotional intensity of the scene
- Layer tension-building sound design underneath
- Cut between interview and b-roll footage
- Music should support emotional weight without overwhelming

### Resolution (2:30 - 3:30)
**Duration**: 1 minute
**Purpose**: Provide closure, reflection, or transition

- Resolution of immediate tension
- Character reflection or insight
- Musical resolution - return to calmer tone
- Prepare viewer for next scene or conclusion

## Asset Groups

### Music
- **setup_music**: Ambient, atmospheric bed for scene establishment (ambient, atmospheric, documentary, calm)
- **tension_music**: Emotional, building score for conflict section (tension, emotional, building, cinematic)
- **resolution_music**: Resolved, calmer score for closure (resolution, calm, reflective, documentary)

### SFX
- **ambient_bed**: Environmental/location sound for texture (ambient, nature, urban, texture)
- **tension_riser**: Subtle tension-building sound effect (riser, tension, subtle, documentary)
- **transition_whoosh**: Smooth transition between sections (whoosh, transition, smooth, cinematic)

### VFX
- **location_title**: Geographic or setting identifier (location_title, documentary, lower_third)
- **date_stamp**: Temporal context if needed (date_stamp, documentary, subtle)

## Cutting Rules

- Follow Setup → Conflict → Resolution structure strictly
- Allow for overlapping transitions between sections (2-3 seconds)
- Preserve natural pauses for documentary authenticity
- Cut to b-roll on nouns and verbs for visual interest
- Keep interview audio continuous when subject is speaking
- Layer ambient sound throughout for environmental immersion
- Total runtime: 3:30 maximum for pacing
